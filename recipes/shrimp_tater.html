Shrimp Saute with Red Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 16oz Red potatoes
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 pint Grape tomatoes halved
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 1/2 lbs large shrimp peeled and deveined
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 5 oz baby spinach
  • 1 1/2 oz finely grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees. Quarter the potatoes and toss them with 2 tsp olive oil, italian seasoning, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet
  2. Roast in the oven for 25-30 min flipping halfway through until golden and tender
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add the remaining olive oil and tomatoes to the skillet. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 3-4 min, or until the tomatoes soften and release some of their juices
  4. Add the garlic, shrimp, and crushed red pepper to the skillet. Sauté the shrimp for 1-2 minutes per side, or until opaque and cooked through.
  5. Add the spinach to the skillet in handfuls, stirring after each addition to allow it to wilt.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and sprinkle half the grated Parmesan cheese over the skillet contents. Stir to combine, allowing the cheese to melt slightly.
  7. Divide the shrimp, sautéed veggies, and spinach between plates. Garnish with the reserved Parmesan cheese and basil ribbons. Serve alongside the roasted red potatoes.
