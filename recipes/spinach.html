Sauteed Spinach

Ingredients

  • Fresh spinach
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrent.
  2. Add the spinach and salt until the spinach is just wilted. About 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Remove the pan from heat and toss the greens with lemon juice. Then season with salt and pepper.
