Broccoli Cheddar Soup

roughly equib=valent to 2 cans of soup

Prep time: 10 minutes, Cook time: 15-20 minutes, Total time: 25-30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 45g unsalted butter
  • 45g all purpose flour
  • 250g (3 cups) finely chopped fresh broccoli
  • 40g (1/3 cup packed) finely grated carrots
  • 10g (2 tsp) neutral oil
  • 360g (1 1/2 cups) low sodium chicken broth
  • 240g (1 cup) milk
  • 80g (1/3 cup) heavy cream
  • (1 tsp divided) Kosher salt
  • (1/2 tsp) Black pepper
  • (3/4 tsp) Garlic powder
  • (3/4 tsp) Onion powder
  • (1/2 tsp) Paprika
  • 120g (1/2 cup) finely shredded mild cheddar cheese
  • 120g (1/2 cup) finely shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Heat neutral oil in a medium pot over medium heat
  2. Add chopped broccoli, grated carrots, and 2g salt
  3. Saute 4 to 5 minutes until the broccoli has softened slightly and the carrots are fragrant
  4. Add butter directly into the pot with the vegetables
  5. Once melted, sprinkle flour over the top
  6. Stir to coat the vegetables and form a thick roux
  7. Cook 2 minutes to remove the raw flour taste
  8. Slowly whisk in chicken broth until smooth with no lumps
  9. Add milk and heavy cream and whisk until fully combined
  10. Stir in remaining salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika
  11. Bring to a gentle simmer on medium low heat
  12. Cook 8 to 10 minutes, stirring often, until the soup is thick, glossy, and the broccoli is tender
  13. Turn heat to low. Add shredded cheese a handful at a time, stirring until completely melted and smooth before adding the next handful
  14. Taste and adjust salt if needed. If it is too thick, whisk in a little extra stock 20g (1 tbsp) at a time. If it is too thin, simmmer another 2 to 3 minutes until it tightens up.
