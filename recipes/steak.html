Sirloin Steak

Ingredients

  • Sirloin steak
  • Canola oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Pat the steak dry with a paper towel and season both sides with salt and pepper and drizzle conola oil over
  2. Preheat the pan medium-high temp then once its warm put the steak in searing both sides for 1 min
  3. Lower the heat so that the butter doesn't burn and add at least 2-3 tablespoons per steak
  4. Add the rosemary and thyme to the pan and while the butter melts pour it over the steak while it cooks
  5. Use a meat thermometer to check the internal temp and take it off the stove when the temperatuer is 5 to 10 less degrees than desirable

Internal temps

  • Rare: 120-130
  • Medium rare: 130-135
  • Medium: 135-145
  • Medium well: 145-155
  • Well done: 155 and up
