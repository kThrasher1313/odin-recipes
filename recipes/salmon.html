Salmon Meal(rice cooker/steamer)

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup brown rice
  • 1 1/8 cup water
  • 1 salmon filet
  • half an asperagus bunch
  • Lemon butter dill sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Pour in rice and water into the rice cooker and push the switch to cook. Cooks for 30-35 min before it switches to warm automatically.
  2. Rip or cut off hard ends of asperagus.
  3. Put salmon filet and asperagus into the steamer, rip or cut asperagus as needed to fit. Steam for 25 min.
  4. Plate salmon, rice, and asperagus. Pour lemon butter dill sauce over the salmon. Season asperagus with salt and pepper.
