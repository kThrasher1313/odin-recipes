Salmon

Use the link below for instructions for rice and steaming salmon or vegetables

Link to instructions for my rice cooker/steamer

Ingredients

  • Salmon
  • Lemon dill sauce or teriyaki sauce and pineapple tidbits

Steps

  1. Coat the bottom of the glass dish with olive oil spray for easier clean up
  2. Place the salmon in the dish skin side down
  3. Pour on sauce and add pineapples if using teriyaki for salmon
  4. Bake the salmon at 425 degrees for 20-25 min
