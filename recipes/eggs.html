Ramen Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 6 1/2 tablespoons white cooking wine
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 table spoon vinegar

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a rolling boil on med-high heat. Lower the eggs in and cook for 7 minutes.
  2. While the eggs are boiling fill a bowl with ice and cold water. After the eggs have finished boiling immediately place the eggs in the ice bath.
  3. Once the eggs are cool enough, peel the eggs.
  4. In a bowl, mix the soy sauce, cooking wine, sugar, and vinegar.
  5. Pour the mixture and place the eggs in a ziplock bag removing the air from it.
  6. Leave the eggs soaking in the marinade for 12-24 hours in the fridge.
  7. Put the eggs in an air tight container in the fridge after marinating. Eat within 2-3 days.
