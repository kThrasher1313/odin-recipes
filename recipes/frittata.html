Frittata

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 10 oz frozen or sauteed spinach
  • 1 slice chopped ham
  • 3 tablespoons chopped onions
  • Shredded sharp cheddar
  • Nutmeg
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl.
  2. Mix all ingredients to a small pot.
  3. Put the pot over medium heat and let it cook until the egg solidifys. About 20 minutes.
  4. Slide onto a plate and cut into slices to serve.
