Artichoke Chicken Pizza

Makes 4 servings

Ingredients

  • Pita bread
  • Artichoke dip
  • Rotisserie chicken
  • Diced cherry tomatoes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees
  2. Place pita bread on parchment paper on a baking sheet
  3. Spread the spinach artichoke dip on liberally on the pita bread leaving a small border around the edge
  4. Scatter the chicken and tomatoes over the top
  5. Bake at 425 degrees for 10-12 minutes, until the dip is bubbling and the bread is crispy
  6. Slice the pita bread and serve warm
