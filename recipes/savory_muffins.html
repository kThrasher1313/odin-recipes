Savory Breakfast Muffins

Serving size: 20-24 muffins

Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
  • (490g) 2 cups plain Greek Yogurt (non-fat or 2% for texture)
  • (120g) 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 lb ground turkey or chicken
  • (360g) 3 cups whole wheat flour (or all-purpose)
  • (10g) 2 tsp baking powder
  • (6g) 1 tsp baking soda
  • (6g) 1 tsp salt
  • (1g) 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 cups fresh chopped spinach
  • (170g) 1.5 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • (75g) 1/2 cup crumbled feta
  • (55g) 1/2 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • (30g) 1/4 cup ground flax seeds (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 F
  2. In a skillet brown the ground meat until fully cooked. Drain any excess fat and set aside to cool slightly
  3. In a large bowl mix together the eggs, greek yogurt, and milk until smooth.
  4. In a seperate bowl mix together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, pepper, and optionally the flax seeds
  5. Fold the dry ingredients into the wet until just combined. Don't overmix or they will get tough
  6. Gently stir in the cooked meat, chopped spinach, cheddar, and feta
  7. Divide the batter into greased muffin tins. Bake for 22-25 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean
  8. Let them cool completely on a wire rack

Tips

  • For a southwest style swap meat for black beans, add corn, and use pepper jack cheese
