Pizza Bagels

Ingredients

  • Bagels
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Shredded mozzerella cheese

Steps

  1. Spread pizza sauce on the open face of the bagel
  2. Sprinkle mozzerella cheese all over the bagel
  3. Place pepperonis around the outside of the bagel
  4. Sprinkle more mozzerella on top of the pepperoni
  5. Place the bagel in the microwave for 1 min or until cheese has melted
