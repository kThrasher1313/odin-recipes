Lemon Poppy Seed Pound Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softe butter (not marg)
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange peel
  • 1/4 cup frozen orange juice concentrate defrosted
  • 4 tablespoons poppyseeds
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 1/4 plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Baking spray and flour for pan

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 350
  2. Cream 1 cup soft butter (not marg) and 2 cups of brown sugar
  3. Add 3 eggs, 1 at a time and beat well. Add 1 tsp vanilla, 1 tablespoon grated orange peel, 1/4 cup frozen orange juice defrosted concentrate, and 4 tablespoons poppyseeds
  4. Add dry ingredients alternately with yogurt. 2 1/2 cups flour, 1 1/4 cups plain yogurt, 1 tsp paking powder, 1 tsp baking soda.
  5. Grease and flour pan then place in overn for 55-60 mins and let cool for 5 minutes
