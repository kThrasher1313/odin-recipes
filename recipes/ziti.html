Baked Ziti

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ziti
  • 1 30 oz jar sauce
  • Small jar ricotta cheese
  • 1 bag shredded mozzerella cheese
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • 1 lb ground beef

Steps

  1. Cook the ziti according to the instructions on the package and drain
  2. Brown the ground beef and add the peppers and onions towards the end
  3. Combine the ziti, peppers, onions, and 1/2 jar of sauce
  4. Spread half the ziti mixture into greased oven safe pan, sprinkle cheese, add rest of ziti mixture, sprinkle more mozzerella and cover with sauce
  5. Bake 375 degrees for 45 min or until bubbly
