Chicken and Ranch Wrap

Ingredients

  • Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • White corn tortillas
  • Shredded colby jack cheese
  • Ranch
  • Shredded lettuce

Steps

  1. If the chicken is thick cut it thinner or pound flatter. Pat the chicken dry then add salt and pepper to it
  2. Heat a skillet with vegetable oil to medium heat, once warm place the chicken in the skillet laying it away from you
  3. Leave the chicken over the oil for about 5-7 min each side until the internal temp is 155 so that carry over cooking makes it safe
  4. Let the chicken rest for 10-20 min until cutting it into cubes.
  5. Wrap the tortillas in a damp paper towel and microwave for 30 sec.
  6. Spread ranch, the cut up chicken, and sprinkle cheese. (microwave if you want the cheese melty), add lettuce on top and serve
