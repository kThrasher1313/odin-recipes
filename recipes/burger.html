Thousand Island Burger

Ingredients

  • Hamburger patty
  • Hamburger bun
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Slice of american cheese
  • Onions
  • Thousand island dressing
  • Pickles
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Season the hamburger patty with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patty in a pan on medium heat. When the burger turns gray/tan over halfway but before 2/3rds flip the burger over.
  3. Cook the other side about a minute less than the first side. Near the end put the slice of cheese on the burger so it melts over it.
  4. Assemble the burger with all the ingredients on top of it.
