Pistachio Salad

Makes 10 servings

Ingredients

  • 3.4 oz pistachio Jell-O instant pudding mix
  • 20 oz can crushed pineapple
  • 1 cup miniature marshmallows
  • 1/2 cup your favorites chopped nuts (pecans, walnuts)
  • 8 oz container Cool Whip (lite-thawed)
  • Cool Whip and cheeries for topping

Steps

  1. Combine pudding mix, pinneapple (with juice), marshmallows and nuts in a large bowl and mix well
  2. Blend in cool whip and chill until ready to serve
  3. Serve with whipped cream and cherries if desired
