Baked potatoes

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Sour cream
  • Shredded cheese
  • Green onions
  • Bacon bits

Steps for oven baked

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Wash and scrub the potato clean
  3. Pat dry and pierce the skin 4-5 times with a fork
  4. Rub the skin with olive oil and sprinkle with salt
  5. Place in the oven on a baking sheet for 50-60 min for a medium to large potato until the skin is crispy and a fork slides in easily
  6. Add toppings

Steps for microwave

  1. Wash and scrub the potato clean
  2. Pat dry and pierce the skin with a fork
  3. Cook for 4-5 min for a medium potato then flip and cook another 2-3 min
  4. Test doneness by sticking a fork into the center
  5. Add toppings

Steps for air fryer

  1. Preheat to 375 degrees
  2. Wash and scrub the potato clean, rub with olive oil and season with salt
  3. Pat dry and pierce the skin with a fork
  4. Cook for 35-45 min, flipping halfway through
  5. Test doneness by sticking a fork into the center
  6. Add toppings
