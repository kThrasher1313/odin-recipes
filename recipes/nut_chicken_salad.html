Walnut and Red Pepper Chicken Salad

4 servings

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked chicken (1-1.5 lbs)
  • 1/2 cup walnuts and pecans
  • 1/2 red pepper
  • 2 tablespoons dill
  • Half bunch of green onions
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 2 tablespoons plain greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Cucumber slices and shredded carrots mixed in or for dipping
  • Bed of mixed greens

Steps

  1. Spread the walnuts and pecans on a dry pan or baking sheet. Toast at 350 degrees in an oven for 5-8 minutes until fragrant. Let them cool completely then chop them
  2. In a large mixing bowl combine the shredded/diced chicken, the chopped nuts, chopped red bell pepper, dill, and green onions
  3. In a seperate small bowl whisk together the mayo, greek yogurt, dijon mustard, and fresh lemon juice for the dressing
  4. Season the dressing generously with salt and black pepper. Stir well until the dressing is smooth and evenly mixed
  5. Pour the prepared dressing over the chicken and vegetable mixture in the large bowl. Stir until evenly coated
  6. Cover the bowl and refridgerate for at least 30min
  7. Serve on a bed of mixed greens for a salad, on bread with lettuce for a sandwich, or as dip with crackers
  8. Add anything extra like carrots and cucumbers in the salad, on the sandwhich, or with the dip for dipping
