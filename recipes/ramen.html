Ramen

Ingredients

  • Pack of instant ramen
  • 1 ramen egg
  • 1 slice of ham
  • Corn
  • Chopped green onions

Steps

  1. Put the ramen in a bowl with water as well as any vegetables if they are frozen
  2. Place the bowl in the microwave and heat for 5 minutes
  3. Without taking the bowl out of the microwave place the slice of ham on top and cover then heat for 1 minute
  4. Pour half of the seasoning packet into the bowl and stir noodles
  5. Cut the egg in half and add in all the other ingredients
