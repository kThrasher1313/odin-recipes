Wonton Soup

4 servings, 15 min prep, 15 min cook, 30 min total

Ingredients

  • 12 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 inch piece fresh ginger sliced thin
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 20 mini frozen wontons
  • 4 baby bok choy, halved lengthwise, and halved
  • bean sprouts
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • scallions, sliced green parts only for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring chicken broth to a boil in a large pot
  2. Smash and slice ginger with the side of a knife to bring out the favor and add to the pot with garlic, cover and cook 5 minutes
  3. Add the bok choy and partially cook 5 minutes, then add the frozen wontons and bean sprouts, simmer according to frozen wonton instructions until wontons are heated through and bok choy is tender and wilted
  4. Stir in soy sauce and sesame oil
  5. Divide soup into four bowls and garnish with fresh scallions
