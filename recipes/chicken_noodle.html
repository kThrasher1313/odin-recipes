Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 chopped yellow onion
  • 2 sliced carrots
  • 2 chopped celery sticks
  • 2 minced cloves garlic
  • 12 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 1 cup shredded cooked chicken breast
  • 2 cup egg noodles
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the oilive oil over medium heat
  2. Add diced onions, sliced carrots, and sliced celery. Saute until softened about 5 min
  3. Stir in minced garlic and saute for an additional 30 sec
  4. Pour in low sodium chicken broth and bring to a simmer
  5. Add shredded cooked chicken, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper. Stir to combine
  6. Simmer for 15-20 min
  7. Bring the soup back up to a boil and pour in the egg noodles cooking for the time on the package until noodles at desired softness
