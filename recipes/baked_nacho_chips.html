Baked Tortilla Chips

Ingredients

  • Paprika
  • Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Stack of thin corn tortillas cut into 6 or 8 triangles
  • Neutral oil (avocado or vegetable)

Steps

  1. In a bowl mix together all the spices and seasonings.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  3. Lightly coat both sides of the triangle tortillas with a neutral oil and season them with the seasoning mix.
  4. Cook them at 350 degrees F for 10-15 minutes until golden brown, flipping them halfway through. Keep a close eye on them after 10 minutes because they could burn easily
  5. As soon as they come out of the oven, toss them in a bowl with the remaining seasoning while they are still hot and slightly oily.
  6. Let them cool completely on a wire rack while they continue to crisp up

Tips

  • If your tortillas are a little bit dry/stale or have been sitting in the fridge they bake up crispier than fresh moist ones
  • You can also cook them in the air fryer at 350 degrees F for about 5-7 minutes shaking the basket every 2 minutes
