Linguini Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1 pound linguini
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2/3 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Pinch of groud nutmeg

Steps

  1. Cook the linguini according to its boxes instructions. Add frozen peas in the last minute of the linguini boiling. Strain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the alfredo sauce by bringing the butter and cream to a boil then immediately lowering the temperature so the mixture gently simmers. Cook the sauce, stirring occasionally, for about 4 minutes or until it leaves a thick coating on the spoon. Stir in the grated cheese, salt, and nutmeg.
  3. Add the sauce to the linguini. Serves 4.
