Southwest Salmon Bowl

5 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro
  • 12 oz frozen or fresh corn
  • 1 Fresh lime
  • Tortilla strips
  • 2-3 lb salmon
  • 15 oz black beans
  • Olive oil
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • Honey
  • garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Pat the salmon dry then drizzle olive oil over it rubbing the oil in with your fingers. In a small bowl mix together chilli powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Sprinkle the mixture generously over the oiled salmon. Gently press the spices into the salmon so they stay put. Grate lime zest over the top of the salmon and cook until 135-140 degrees F.
  3. Cook the quinoa according the instructions
  4. Chop the red bell pepper, red onion, and cilantro. Mix them all together in a bowl
  5. Mix the quinoa with the corn and black beans
  6. When the salmon is done pull it out of the oven and squeeze fresh lime juice over it immediately. Let the salmon sit for 10 min before flaking it with a fork
  7. Assemble with the quinoa, corn, and black bean mixture on the bottom. Salmon in the middle. Cilantro, red bell pepper, and red onion on top.
  8. In a small bowl mix together the dressing as 3 tbsp olive oil, 2 tbsp fresh lime juice, 1 tsp honey, 1/2 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, and salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Top with tortilla stips and the dressing
