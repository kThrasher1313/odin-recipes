Honey Garlic Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • 2.5 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter divided
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • Chopped green onions
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cut raw chicken into 1 inch cubes
  2. Put the chicken in a bowl and cover with salt and pepper and stir
  3. Add two tablespoons of flour and stir until the chicken is fully coated
  4. In a small bowl, combine 1 tablespoon of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of white vinegar, and 2.5 tablespoons of honey to create your sauce.
  5. On medium heat add 1 tablespoon of olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter to a pan
  6. Place the chicken in the pan cooking the pieces about 4-5 min per side
  7. Move the chicken to the edge of the pan and in the middle add 1 tablespoon of butter, 2 tablespoons of garlic and sautee for 30s or until fragrent
  8. Add the sauce to the pan and turn the heat to medium-low and coat the chicken in the sauce. Continue cooking until the sauce thickens about 2-3 min
  9. Serve with rice, add chopped green onions and sesame seeds
