Black Bean Burger

serving: 8 burgers

Ingredients

  • 30 ounce can black beans, , drained and rinsed
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1.33 cup whole wheat breadcrumbs
  • 2 Tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 8 hamburger buns
  • desired burger toppings: lettuce, tomato, etc.

Steps

  1. Place rinsed and drained black beans in a bowl. If needed, blot them with a paper towel to remove extra moisture. Place in a large bowl and mash well with a fork.
  2. Use a food processor to mine the bell pepper, onion and garlic. Transfer veggies to a fine mesh strainer and use the back of a spoon to press out excess moisture. (If it's too wet, the burger wont hold together as well.)
  3. Add the strained vegetable mixture to the bowl with the black beans. Add the chili powder, cumin and salt and pepper and stir to combine. Add beaten egg and mix. Stir in bread crumbs.
  4. Scoop mixture into 4 even sized patties, forming them and pressing flat with your hands.
  5. Cook over medium heat in a greased grill pan or skillet for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  6. Serve burgers on a bun or lettuce wrap and top with a spoonful of chipotle mayo, lettuce, tomato, cheese, avocado, or any desired toppings

Other cooking methods

  • To Grill: Cook on well oiled grill over medium heat, for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  • To Cook in Oven: Bake at 375 degrees F on a lightly oiled baking sheet, for 8-10 minutes on each side.
