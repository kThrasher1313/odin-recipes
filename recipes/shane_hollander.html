Shane Hollander Drink

Ingredients

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 3/4 oz of lime juice
  • 1/2 oz of maple syrup
  • Ginger ale
  • Fresh mint and lime to garnish
  • Cream of coconut for the rim

Steps

  1. Mix together the vodka, lime juice, and maple syrup. Pour into a glass.
  2. Top off the rest with ginger ale
  3. Garnish with fresh mint and lime
  4. Rim the glass with cream of coconut
