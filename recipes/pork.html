Breaded Pork Chops

Ingredients

  • Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • Pork Chops
  • Eggs
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 F
  2. Dab the pork chops with a paper towel to remove extra moisture.
  3. Dredge (lightly coat) the pork chops in flour and then dip in egg. Add porkchops to the bread crumbs and gently press the crumbs so they stick.
  4. Brown the pork chops in a skillet to crisp the crumbs.
  5. Place the pork chops evenly on parchment lined tray.
  6. Bake 3/4" chops (that have been browned) at 425 F for about 12-14 minutes or until the breaded pork chops reach 145 F using a meat thermometer.
