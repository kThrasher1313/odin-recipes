Poached Egg

Ingredients

  • White Vinegar
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Toast

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a gentle boil (not a rolling boil)
  2. Add a little bit of white vinegar to the pot to keep the egg proteins strong
  3. Crack the egg into a small bowl
  4. Stir the pot of water to make a whirlpool and gently lower the egg into the whirlpool so the whites wrap around themselves
  5. Once the egg whites are no longer see through use a spoon to gently lift the egg from the bottom so that it doesn't stick
  6. Let the egg cook for a few min, you can tell if its done if the outside is bouncy to the touch and the insides are still liquid
  7. Serve on toast and season with salt and pepper
